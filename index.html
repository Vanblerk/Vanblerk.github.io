About us

WHEN THE GOING GETS TOUGH, THE TOUGH CALL PEPI

Johann completed his LLB studies at the University of Pretoria in 2011. In 2012 he enrolled to complete his post graduate studies in Insolvency law while attending law school simultaneously, obtaining his master’s degree in Insolvency Law at the end of 2013. During his studies, he quickly realized that he had an interest in insolvency law and related corporate law. Whilst completing his master’s degree, he commenced his articles as candidate attorney at the infamous Danie Potgieter Attorneys, a specialist insolvency firm, and was admitted as an Attorney of the High Court in March 2014. During 2020, Johann decided to start his own law firm after the passing of his mentor and registered JJ Pepler Incorporated.

Johann prides himself on SERVICE! He is very professional and provides uncompromised legal service to his clients, ensuring client satisfaction and peace of mind. He has been exposed to business from a young age and has experience far beyond his years. Insolvency law encompasses a broad spectrum of law and his academic knowledge together with years of expertise in this area provide a solid foundation to give accurate advice and assistance to clients. Some of his specialties include, but is not limited to, Liquidation, Sequestration, Rehabilitation and Trust law and he is often consulted by colleagues regarding the complexities of these legal spheres.

He enrolled and completed his Conveyancing certification during 2019, being admitted as a conveyancer in October 2019. He is currently busy with his Notarial Practice certification. Watch this space.
