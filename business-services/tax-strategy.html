Tax Strategy

The balancing act between tax and protection

TAX STRATEGY

Tax strategy forms an essential part of corporate structuring. In order to maximize company profitability, you must minimize tax liability. The taxman has over the last few years implemented several new taxes, increased others and legislation has been put in place to effectively collect taxes. Companies need an effective tax strategy to legally minimize the amount of taxes it pays in order to stay profitable in an ever-increasing difficult economy.


TAX EVASION vs TAX AVOIDANCE

An effective tax strategy is not an evasion of taxes. It is the legal mitigation of tax liability. We know the difference.


wHERE TO START

Consult qualified persons and/or specialist attorneys when considering your tax strategy. This area of the law is like a minefield and one must proceed with the utmost caution. Fortunately, we have years of experience assisting clients, both individuals and companies, with this process. The options available are case specific and contingent on the client, nature of the business and tax protocols applicable to each case. However, the aim is always to mitigate any tax liability in a manner that is lawful, ethical and effective.


TAX BURDEN

The ever-increasing burden on the private sector has an immediate and definite effect on the economy. This, in turn, puts more pressure on companies which in turn puts more pressure on SARS to collect taxes.
