Tax relief applications

Temporary / Permanent tax write-off

TAX RELIEF APPLICATION DEFINITION

A tax relief application is an application whereby the South African Revenue Services (SARS) can temporarily or permanently write-off all or a portion of the taxes due to it. If a company is profitable except for the tax liability, one can approach SARS for tax relief.


BASIS FOR APPLICATION

Should a company be profitable except for the tax liability, SARS can compromise on the taxes due to it. It should be in SARS’s future interest to compromise on taxes. The main goal is to keep a taxpayer alive and retain the business, employment, the skills and economic benefit for South Africa.


FORMAT OF APPLICATION

There are numerous aspects that need to be covered in such an application before SARS can legally entertain the application. Should SARS not be informed of all the requirements under the Tax Act, the application is substantially flawed, and SARS may not grant temporary or permanent relief. We deal with all formalities as required by the Act.


TAX RELIEF

SARS may grant a temporary or permanent write-off of a percentage of the tax liability. Call us now to find out more.
