Asset Protection

Hold on to your assets. You’ve earned it

what is ASSET PROTECTION?

Asset protection is a set of legal techniques within the legislative and common law framework dealing with protecting assets of individuals and business entities from civil money judgments. The goal of asset protection planning is to insulate assets from claims of creditors without perjury or tax evasion. The aim is to legally safeguard assets against unforeseen occurrences such as debt, divorce, liquidation, sequestration, execution by the sheriff and any other unforeseen circumstances”

Consult qualified persons and/or specialist attorneys when considering asset protection and/or estate planning. This area of the law is like a minefield and one must proceed with the utmost caution. Fortunately, we have years of experience assisting clients, both individuals and companies, with this process. The numerous instruments available to protect assets are case specific and contingent on the client, nature of the assets and tax protocols applicable to those assets. However, the aim is always to protect the assets in a manner that is lawful, ethical and effective.
